Two guy problem

Max Kovalovs · 2018/12/28 · 1 minute read

There are 2 people in this riddle - person A and person B. Person B is positioned vertically below person A. Person A starts walking horizontally to the right and person B starts walking directly at person A at all times. The initial distance between them is d and they are walking at the same speed.

Figure 1: The two guy problem.

  • Will person B ever reach person A? Why/Why not?
  • What will the eventual distance (d’ in the figure) between them be? In other words, what will the distance between them tend to?
